ABOUT US

Everything in one place.

The coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has now spread across the entire globe and the recovery of the hospitality industry will likely take a while. It is already clear that coronavirus can spread through touching contaminated surfaces. This puts establishments such as your restaurant in the spot as most of the surface is shared by different people every day. While cleaning and disinfecting tables and bars a good start, there is still one thing that people need and share; menus. So how can you eliminate the sharing of physical menus between customers? The answer is simple, replace the menu with a PDF QR Code!

What's made EaZy with us?

Contactless Menu

Contactless menus feature QR codes that customers point and scan with their smartphone's camera, taking them to an online digital menu where they can browse food and drink options.

Hassle-free Ordering

We simplify the process of ordering food by checking out the menu availble. Take advantage of our Contactless Menu and save time with out hassle-free ordering!

Contactless Payment

Contactless Payments have been around for a while. This eliminates the exchange of the card between consumer and employee as well as the need for the consumer to touch the card reader.

Reservations

Allows restaurants to control how many guests arrive and when, which will be especially important for reduced seating which will mean flipping a table takes longer than it used to.
